The Guy Behind the Shades

My name is Aram and I want to live in a world where security comes first, authentication is more convenient and coffee is in endless supply. When I'm not learning how the reverse engineer the latest smart toaster you can find me learning about how machine learning can be utilized to train software to detect advanced threats.

After learning Web Design and Development during in my undergrad years I wanted to take things a step further and learn how to program and also secure the sites I was developing. After starting my Master’s program in Cyber Security Engineering at the University of San Diego I decided to start this blog to outline my journey. In it you can find what I've have learned in and out of the classroom. There are also white papers, tutorials, thoughts, curiosities, fascinations and ideas relating to Cyber Security and how it affects our society.
